Footlab Turf Rules

  • Absolutely no metal studs; turf shoes only.
  • No food or glass bottles on the pitch.
  • Respect teammates and opponents; zero-tolerance for rough behavior.
  • Booking must be confirmed 1 hour before kick-off.
  • Warm-up area available; maintain the turf surface.
  • Insurance and liability in plain text with each confirmation.
  • Referees and first-aid ready for competitive play.
